<h2>Welcome</h2>
Join host, Greg O'Grady, and his guests for a weekly dose of speech, language and what it's like to live with a communication impairment. Season 3 starts May 8!  Don't forget to follow us on our [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/somestutterpodcast/) and TikTok (coming soon) pages for the latest news and discussions.
